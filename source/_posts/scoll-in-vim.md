## move your cursor

press 'j' or 'k' are able to move up or down

## scroll

- 'ctrl' + 'f'(forward)
- backward: 'ctrl' + 'b'

## half scroll

- up: 'ctrl' + 'u'
- down: 'ctrl' + 'd'

## jump to the end

'G'
